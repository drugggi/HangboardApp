Grips & Grades hangboard application is finger training application for every climber who likes to hangboard or does not always have time to go to the gym. Grips & Grades main focus is grade based hangboard workouts from 5A to 8B. Every hold and grip is graded from 1 (jug) upwards and the application randomly chooses hold combinations for a given grade. Main screen offers five premade workout structures (20,35,50,65)min and "Test progression", where the workout goes from the easiest hang to the hardest. The application of course provides full customization of holds, grips and time control settings.
